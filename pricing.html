Frequently Asked

Is there a minimum contract? +

I recommend a 3-month minimum to see meaningful SEO results. SEO is a long-term investment — most significant results appear in months 3–6.

Do you guarantee rankings? +

No one can guarantee exact rankings — Google's algorithm is the final decision-maker. I do guarantee transparent work, consistent effort, and data-backed strategies that consistently produce results.

What's included in the audit? +

Technical analysis, keyword gaps, competitor benchmarking, content audit, backlink profile review, and a prioritised action plan — all in a detailed PDF report.

Can I upgrade/downgrade plans? +

Absolutely. You can change plans at the end of any month with 15 days notice. No penalty, no lock-in.
